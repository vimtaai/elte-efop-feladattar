Monday

Hungarian name: hétfő, which literally means head of the week.

Tuesday

Hungarian name: kedd, which originates from the Hungarian word for two, that being kettő.

Wednesday

Hungarian name: szerda, which originates from various Slavic words for middle, like sreda or srednji.

Thursday

Hungarian name: csütörtök, which originates from various Slavic words meaning fourth day of the week, like četvrtok

Friday

Hungarian name: péntek, which originates from various Slavic words meaning fifth day of the week, like petok .

Saturday

Hungarian name: szombat, which originates from the Hebrew word Shabbat, which is Judaism's day of rest on the seventh day of the week - even though in Hungary it is only the sixth day, as Saturday is the last one.

Sunday

Hungarian name: vasárnap, which almost literally means market day - that being vásár nap precisely.

Week

Hungarian name: hét, which literally means seven - that being used in everyday language for the number 7 as well.
